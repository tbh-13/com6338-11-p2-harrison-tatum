This is a text-based adventure game entitled, 'The Disappearing Forest." 

The pseudocode breaks down the creation of this site, starting from the interactive scenes to the rendering of the scene and start of game function, including clearing previous scene/buttons and moving to the next scene/button options. 

This adventure contains 4 scenes, each with two separate button options. One option will continue the story, the other option will end your story, prompting you to try again. Ultimately, you are trying to reach the 'dreamland.' If you choose the wrong option, your story will end and you will not reach the final scene.
